Overview

NC Chase is a live-action chase game. Everyone is trying to evade being caught,
and trying to catch their own runner.
Every morning, you will receive an email with your player ID, and your runner's name & Form Class.
Your mission is to identify, track down, and catch your runner by placing a clothing peg on them.

In the same way that you're chasing someone, someone is also chasing you!
So, to survive you'll need to play both offensively and defensively to make it through the week.
Your team is your house, who will never be chasing you.
