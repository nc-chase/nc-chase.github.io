NC Chase 23

Tags

Tagging

To catch your runner, clip a clothes peg to their clothing (not their bag).
Once you have done this, obtain their 'player ID' (by asking).
Submit the tag by completing the caught form with your and your runners player ID.

Being tagged

If you have been tagged:

Provide your player ID to your chaser.
Do not chase after your runner as it will not count as a tag.

Getting back in

To get back into NC Chase 23, you will need to find QR codes around the school
By scanning this QR code and completing the form, if you were quick enough, you will be in by tomorrow
