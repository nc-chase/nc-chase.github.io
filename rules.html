NC Chase 23

Rules

General

The game only runs before school, interval & lunch.
You may run away and prevent your Chaser from catching you.
Your assigned runner is changed each day (even if you don't catch them).
Every player is also the runner to another player.

Rules

No breaking school rules.
No one should ever have to use force. Doors should not be busted down etc.
Attaching a peg to a bag does NOT count.
Do not play in the hall, LSC or Office.
Clubs are safe spaces - please don't interrupt club activities.

Most importantly, use your common sense when playing. If a space doesn't seem appropriate to play in, don't.
If you do suspect cheating / unfair play, contact: gamemaster@newlands.school.nz
